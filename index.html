What I Offer

Since the 21st century is the age of internet, everything that isn't online is like non-existent. Having a brilliant web presentation of your company can attract many more customers while having a bad one or none can decrease your impact. I'd like to offer you to make a professional web presentation for your business or create an eshop, that can really catch someone's eye and looks exactly according to your wishes. Mostly, I work with WordPress, Wix or Shopify. However, I've also learned to code in HTML, CSS and Javascript along with modern responsive web design frameworks like Bootstrap and React.In addition, I can work with graphic programs and edit videos in order to prepare them for the website. Furthermore, I can communicate in multiple languages such as English, Czech, French and basic German and Swedish and I'm very open to work in an international group of people. I'm constantly learning and developing my skills to be able to deliver better results and keep up with new technologies.

About

Let me introduce myself, my name is Veronika and I'm a starting self-studied front developer from Prague. I have studied Bachelor's degree in Biochemistry and Ecology at university, however recently I took up web development and I study it almost everyday. I believe that the best thing about it is how versatile it can be. Primarily, I study from online sources like Freecodecamp which is an excellent project that makes IT education much more available for everyone. I think I can say about myself that I'm a very hard-working, flexible and tolerant person. Apart from web development, among my interests are reading, nature, learning languages, traveling and I like to learn about cybersecurity, which is essential these days. I also support the rights of short people that are often disregarded despite being a strange separate minority.
I'd love to help you with setting up your eshop, business or personal website.
